Our Services

  • Lessons

    We offer lessons for all ages and all levels of riding, beginners through advanced!. We have many different horses available for lessons, wether you have never ridden a horse before or have been riding for years. Our Head Instructor, Jill Bowden, is amazing at matching up horse and rider, and making sure both are comfortable and able to succeed together. Contact us today to set up your first lesson or find out more info on pricing.

  • Boarding

    Boarding is full service at Twisdenwood. We offer high quality grain twice daily and hay is available to the horses 24 hours a day. All day turnout is included, weather permitting. Group or Individual turnout is available. We have both an indoor and outdoor. Our barn is not heated, but is extremely well insulated, staying at an average of 50 degrees even in the middle of winter. We have two heated viewing rooms available, with a kitchen in one of them. Miles of trails to explore right on property.

  • Summer Camp

    Twisdenwood II offers multiple weeks of summer camps throughout the summer. Kids aged 6 - 12 can learn to ride as well as learn how to care for horses during a full week of day camp.   • IEA Team

    Twisdenwood II participates in IEA Zone 1 Region 2. Students who take consistent lessons and are in grades 4 - 12 can participate in either our middle school or highschool huntseat riding team. Contact Twisdenwood II or visit rideiea.org for more information!

  • Horse Showing

    Twisdenwood attends many shows throughout the show season, both locally and across New England. Check out this page for updates on our 2020 show season schedule.
